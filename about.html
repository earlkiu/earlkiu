About

I see beauty in people, where every living being denotes a charming nature, concocting beautiful moments between scenes — like characters in an unfolding story. Complex yet vivid, acting their best as if vying for an Emmy nomination.

Photography lets me hold those moments — where poise meets rawness, where simplicity carries depth. I'm drawn to quiet expression, sculpted light, and the honesty of presence.

Currently working in-house, but open to meaningful collaborations that align with this rhythm.
